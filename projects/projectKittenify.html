Kitten-ify

Everything is better as a kitten



Kitten-ify is a chrome extension that, on click, turns all images on the page into a random kitten image!

Download

How to load extension

Download the Kitten-ify extension folder using the button above and unzip

V

Navigate to chrome://extensions

V

Toggle Developer mode in the top right

V

Click 'Load unpacked', in the top left

Click the leftmost button

V

Select the Kitten-ify folder that was downloaded

V

Pin the extension; Use it by clicking the icon on a webpage

V

Enjoy the kittens!

Notices

Due to restriction, it's possible that certain images are not replaced due to the density of images present on a page, most apparent when quickly scrolling through google images. Avoid high density image pages for best results.
